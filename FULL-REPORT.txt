SECTION A: AGENCY EARNINGS MATH FIX
===============================================================

ISSUE: Owner earnings for CPM_SPLIT campaigns were calculated as
raw views x ownerCpm, ignoring the maxPayoutPerClip cap. A clip
with 333K views and $1 ownerCpm showed $333.50 owner earnings
instead of $100 (proportional to the capped clipper earnings).

FILE CHANGED: src/lib/earnings-calc.ts
WHAT I DID: Updated calculateOwnerEarnings() to accept optional
clipperGrossEarnings and clipperCpm parameters. When provided,
uses proportional calculation: clipperEarnings * (ownerCpm / clipperCpm).
Falls back to raw views * ownerCpm when cap context unavailable.

FILE CHANGED: src/app/api/clips/[id]/review/route.ts
WHAT I DID: Updated the AgencyEarning creation on clip approval
to pass breakdown.grossClipperEarnings and clipperCpm to
calculateOwnerEarnings.

FILE CHANGED: src/lib/tracking.ts
WHAT I DID: Same fix in the tracking cron — passes
breakdown.grossClipperEarnings and clipperCpm.

VERIFIED BY TRACING CODE? Yes.
Math trace: 333500 views, $1 clipperCpm, $100 maxPayout cap.
clipperGrossEarnings = $100 (capped). ownerAmt = 100 * (1/1) = $100.
Correct.
