1. Vercel cron schedule
   File: vercel.json

2. Tracking nextCheckAt — round to clean hour marks
   File: src/lib/tracking.ts

   Added roundToNextSlot(intervalMin) helper:
   - 60 min → next round hour (5:37 PM → 6:00 PM)
   - 120 min → next even hour (5:37 PM → 6:00 PM, 6:05 PM → 8:00 PM)
   - 240 min → next 4-hour mark (midnight, 4AM, 8AM, noon, 4PM, 8PM)
   - 480 min → next 8-hour mark (midnight, 8AM, 4PM)
   - 1440 min → next midnight UTC
   - 4320 min → midnight UTC + 3 days

   Replaced: new Date(Date.now() + newInterval * 60 * 1000)
   With: roundToNextSlot(newInterval)

   Error retry (30 min) unchanged — still uses raw offset since it's a retry, not a scheduled slot.

   How it works with hourly cron:
   - Cron fires at :00 every hour
   - Picks up all jobs where nextCheckAt <= now
   - After processing, each job's nextCheckAt is set to a clean future hour mark
   - Jobs naturally align to the cron's hourly cadence
