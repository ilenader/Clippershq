Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

When you sign in with Discord, we receive your Discord username, email address, and profile picture. When you use the platform, we collect clip URLs you submit, view counts from public platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube), earnings data, and wallet addresses for payouts.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to operate the platform: calculating earnings based on clip views, processing payout requests, communicating with you about campaigns and account status, and improving the service.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share data only with service providers necessary to operate the platform (database hosting via Supabase, email via Resend, clip tracking via Apify).

Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication (keeping you logged in). We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at noreply@clipershq.com or through our Discord server.
