Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you sign in with Discord, we receive your Discord username, email address, avatar image, and Discord user ID. When you use the platform, we collect:

  • Social media account URLs and usernames you link
  • Clip URLs you submit
  • View counts, likes, comments, and shares from public platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube)
  • Earnings and payout data
  • Wallet addresses for crypto payouts
  • IP addresses for security and fraud prevention
  • Chat messages sent within the platform

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data for: operating the platform, calculating earnings based on clip views, processing payout requests, detecting and preventing fraud, communicating with you about campaigns and account status via in-app notifications and email, improving the service, and maintaining platform security.

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties. We share data only with service providers necessary to operate the platform:

  • Discord (authentication)
  • Supabase (database hosting and file storage)
  • Resend (transactional email delivery)
  • Apify (public clip stat tracking)
  • Vercel (application hosting)

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies only. This includes a NextAuth session cookie for authentication and a PKCE verification cookie for secure Discord OAuth flow. We do not use advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking cookies.

5. Data Retention

Your account data is kept while your account is active. If you request deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days. Aggregate anonymized data (total platform views, earnings statistics) may be retained.

6. Your Rights

You can request a copy of your data, request correction of inaccurate data, or request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at noreply@clipershq.com.

7. Updates

We may update this policy with notice via the platform or email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance. By using the platform, you consent to this policy.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact noreply@clipershq.com or reach us through our Discord server.
