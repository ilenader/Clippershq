Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Essential Cookies Only

Clippers HQ uses essential cookies required for the platform to function. These are strictly necessary and cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality.

Cookies We Use

NextAuth Session Cookie: Keeps you logged in. Set when you sign in with Discord. Expires when you sign out or after the session timeout.

PKCE Verification Cookie: A short-lived cookie used during the Discord OAuth sign-in process to prevent security attacks. Automatically deleted after login completes.

No Advertising or Tracking Cookies

We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, analytics cookies, or third-party tracking cookies. We do not track you across other websites.

Third-Party Services

Our Calendly booking widget (on the brands page) may set its own cookies when you interact with it. These are controlled by Calendly and subject to their own cookie policy.

Managing Cookies

You can clear or block cookies through your browser settings. Note that blocking essential cookies will prevent you from signing in and using the platform.

Contact

Questions about our cookie practices can be directed to noreply@clipershq.com.
